This scented story begins in the year 2017. A new era was about to begin in the city of Windhoek. A new era where Fragrances would be available to all, quality fragrances that people could use and enjoy!

Our humble beginnings began with the dream of creating a luxury boutique-style environment where fragrance lovers could discover the joy of quality fragrances.

The Lomefragrance is a Namibian company that was established 5 years ago. It has grown to become one of Namibia's leading stockists of internationally inspired luxurious fragrances. We pride ourselves on providing fantastic service, a unique store experience and access to the largest range of fragrances in Namibia.
